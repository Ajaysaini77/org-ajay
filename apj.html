A P J Abdul kalam

About A.P.J. Abdul kalam

A P J ABdul kalam, the "Missile man" , was born on october 15, 1931 , in Rameswaram. Tamil Nadu, He server as the 11th President of India and was a renowned scientist, known for his work in India's space and missile programs.

Achivement

  • Developed india's first satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III).
  • Lead India's successful nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Wrote books like "Wings of Fire" and "India 2020".
  • Received the bharat Ratn, India's highest civilian award.
